Send Feedback

The preferred way to give feedback is via e-mail to the author or filing an issue or suggestion on the project issue list, because feedback sent that way can turn into a valuable two-way discussion. Feedback or suggestions provided via the issue list can also be seen by others who can offer their support for the suggestion and join in with their ideas of the merits of design approaches to achieving the desired outcome (and for bug reports they can add their own insight or background to the search for a fix).

However, for people who do not want to use a Google account or who wish to main strictly anonymous, this form provides an alternative way of providing feedback to the project author; any message posted here will be saved and the project author notified, but no-one else will be able to see what is sent.

All kinds of feedback, whether positive or negative, suggestions or complaints, is welcomed and encouraged and will be taken seriously. It may help if you want to look at some of the existing issues to frame what you would like to say, but by all means don't feel constrained by that.

Although the project author is notified whenever feedback is sent, please bear in mind that between timezones and other work, not every item will be looked at immediately; however, be assured that it will be looked at.

If you want to give feedback specifically about the filter rules, then an alternative and more specific way of doing that is to use the rule upload page (also available from the Custom profile page in the Steam Limiter), particularly if you have some specific suggestion you'd like to make to improve one of the filter rules.
